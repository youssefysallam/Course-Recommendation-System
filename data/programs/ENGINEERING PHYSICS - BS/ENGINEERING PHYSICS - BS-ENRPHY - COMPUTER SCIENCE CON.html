NO = Requirement Incomplete

IP = Grade for Course In Progress
P/F = Grade for Optional Pass/Fail Course In Progress
TR = Transfer Grade prior to Spring 2011
T** = Transfer Grade after Spring 2011

>D = Duplicated Course
>R = Repeatable Course
RP = Repeated Course - Credit Preserved
>S = Course with Credit Split between Requirements
>X = Repeated Course - Credit Reduced
>- = Course with Credit Reduced

FL = Fall | WR = Winter | SP = Spring | SU = Summer
